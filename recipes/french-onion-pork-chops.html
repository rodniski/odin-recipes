SLOW COOCKER FRENCH ONION PORK CHOPS

A lighter version of a wonderful main dish. Easy to prepare and add some quick sides to. Yummy!

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Cook: 8hrs 15mins
  • Total: 8hrs 25min
  • Serving: 5
  • Yeld: 5

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 5 (6 ounce) pork chops
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
  • 1 (18.5 ounce) can French onion soup (such as ProgressoÂ®)
  • 1 (8 ounce) container light sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Preparation mode

  1. Spray a skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat. Quickly brown pork chops in the skillet, seasoning with garlic salt, 5 to 7 minutes total. Transfer to a slow cooker and pour French onion soup on top.
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 8 hours.
  3. Remove the chops and keep warm. Add sour cream and flour to the slow cooker, mix well, and warm until slightly thickened, 10 to 15 minutes. Serve sauce over the chops.
